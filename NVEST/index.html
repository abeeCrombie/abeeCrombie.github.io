HODL; Do You Know Where Your Going?

Navigating the cryptocurrency landscape is a daunting task. New tokens are created daily, prices move rapidly, sentiment can swing wildly and major project advancements occur frequently. In order to stay on top of these issues and more one must be prepared to analyze large amounts of data as the answers are not always straight forward. Indeed, a smart investor needs to find the signal from the noise. But how do you find that signal in a world awash with data?

TLDR --avoid the data overload! At nvest.ai, we are building a platform to help investors and traders in crypto markets analyze all of the data that is out there. Our system, built by a team of financial pros, PhD's and domain experts, uses sophistacted AI (including Deep learning and modern NLP) to mine data and transform it into knowledge. From newbies to experts, our tools will empower your decision making process.

Your not alone. Connect with like-minded investors/traders to see what they are up to and share of information easily. Nvest.ai is also a testbed for data scientists and developer contributions. With tailored made tools to easily launch your own apps, (and be rewarded with nvest tokens) we can create a large ecosystem and deliver better tools than any centralized organization.
